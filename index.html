About me 

Hi, I'm Adela! 

I'm a photographer, model and everything in between. I'm based in Bucharest, Romania, but I love to travel, have fun, meet amazing people and take amazing photos. 

I'm here to show you some candid photos for when you're missing home. I want to give you a meaningful and authentic experience, full of emotions, laughs and creativity. 

Let's capture everyday reality together and let ourselves be inspired by madness, creativity and nostalgia!
